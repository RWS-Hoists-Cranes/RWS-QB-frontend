RWS QUICKBOOKS Privacy Policy

Last updated: 9/24/2025. Contact: sales@rws.com

What we collect

  • QuickBooks realmId, access and refresh tokens
  • Data needed for features you enable (e.g., Customers, Invoices, Items)
  • Error logs without sensitive financial details

How we use data

  • Provide features you request
  • Troubleshoot and improve the service
  • Legal compliance

Sharing

  • No selling of data
  • Processors only, under contract (hosting, logging)

Security

  • HTTPS in transit, encrypted tokens at rest, access restricted

Retention

  • Delete stored QuickBooks data within 30 days after disconnect or written request

Your choices

  • Disconnect any time
  • Request copy or deletion via email

Children

Not intended for users under 13.

Changes
